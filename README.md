# Hi there, I'm Gary Ritchie

**Designer & 3D Artist — 25+ years crafting visual experiences across VFX, XR, UX/UI, and interactive media.**

---

## About Me

I'm a Greater Pittsburgh-based designer and creative technologist with over 25 years of experience at the intersection of art and technology. My work spans film/TV VFX, full-dome planetarium productions, VR/AR experiences, corporate UX, and interactive web platforms. I'm passionate about using design to make complex information intuitive and beautiful.

I believe great design lives at the crossroads of user needs, business goals, and creative craft — and I've spent my career building that bridge.

## What I Focus On

- **UX/UI Design** — Interface design, wireframing, prototyping, usability research
- **3D Art & VFX** — Full-dome animation, environmental design, visual effects
- **Creative Technology** — WebGL, Three.js, Blender, Unreal Engine
- **Corporate Communications** — Design systems, multi-channel storytelling, brand identity

## Selected Projects

### [Planetarium: Exoplanets](https://planetariumhamburg.de) — Full-Dome VFX Production
> Subcontracted for Glen Johnson (Planet Development) — exhibited at Planetarium Hamburg. Created 3D assets, environments, and visual effects for an immersive planetarium experience.

### [Blueprints to Blastoff](https://www.carniescience.org) — Full-Dome Animation
> Produced full-dome animation and VFX for Carnegie Science Center featuring rocket launches, ISS flybys, and lunar/Mars landings. Used Unreal Engine for the production pipeline.

### [ISM Services VR Platform](https://ism-services.com) — Data-Driven VR Product UI
> Designed the user interface for a VR product platform, translating complex data visualization into intuitive spatial experiences.

### [IBACOS Intranet & Design System](https://ibacos.com) — Corporate Design & UX
> Led the design of interactive standards, web applications, and technical content for a Fortune 500 organization. Created scalable design systems spanning intranet, documentation, and multimedia.

### [Lyon Ritchie Portfolio](https://lyonritchie.com) — Personal Studio
> Ongoing creative studio work spanning interactive design, 3D art, motion graphics, and client projects for government and commercial audiences.
